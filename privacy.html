Foundry Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 July 2026

Foundry is a workout tracking app. This policy explains what it stores, where, and your choices. It is written to be read, not to hide behind legalese.

The short version

Foundry works fully on your device. If you choose to create an account, your data also syncs to a private cloud database so it survives across devices. There are no ads, no analytics or tracking SDKs, and your data is never sold or shared with advertisers. You can export or permanently delete everything at any time.

What is stored

If you use Foundry without an account, everything stays in your device's local storage and nothing is sent anywhere.

If you create an account, the following is stored in our cloud database (hosted by Supabase):

  • Your email address, used only to sign in and to send password-reset emails.
  • Your training data: workouts, exercises, weights, reps, cardio sessions, body measurements, bodyweight, personal records, plans, programs, and app settings.
  • Your display name and summary weekly stats (total volume, session count, streak), but only if you turn on "Share stats with friends." This is the only data other users can see. It is off by default.

Foundry does not collect your location, contacts, device identifiers, or any advertising data, and contains no third-party tracking or analytics.

Who can see your data

Your training data is private to your account, enforced at the database level by row-level security: no other user can read it. The only exception is the summary leaderboard stats described above, and only when you opt in. Turning sharing back off removes you from the friends board.

Where it is processed

Cloud data is stored and processed by Supabase, Inc., our database and authentication provider, acting on our behalf. Purchases (the optional tip) are processed by Apple; Foundry never sees your payment details.

Your choices and controls

  • Export: Settings lets you export your full data and measurement history as files at any time.
  • Reset: "Reset All Data" erases your data on the device and in the cloud while keeping your account.
  • Delete account: "Delete Account" permanently removes your account and all associated data from the cloud. This cannot be undone.
  • Use offline: skip account creation entirely and keep all data on your device.

Data retention

Cloud data is kept until you delete it. Reset clears your training data; Delete Account removes the account and all its data. Deletion is immediate and permanent.

Children

Foundry is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, the date above will be updated. Continued use after a change means you accept the revised policy.
